JURISDICTION: New York
COURT: Supreme Court
YEAR: 2018

Evidence at trial established that, in contemplation of the matrimonial action, the husband transferred substantial marital funds to accounts held in his sole name without fair consideration. The court further finds that these transfers constituted a wasteful dissipation of marital assets. In fashioning an equitable distribution, the court weighs heavily the improper encumbrance and dissipation of marital property by the husband.
