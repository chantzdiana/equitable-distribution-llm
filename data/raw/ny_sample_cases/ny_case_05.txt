JURISDICTION: New York
COURT: Supreme Court
YEAR: 2022

The court finds that the husband committed acts of domestic violence against the wife during the marriage, the effects of which were both prolonged and severe. In determining an equitable distribution of marital property, the court considers the nature and impact of such conduct. The court further relies on its authority to consider any other factor it expressly finds to be just and proper in order to achieve an equitable result.
