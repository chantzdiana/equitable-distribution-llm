JUDGE: Smith

During the marriage, the wife made substantial direct and indirect contributions to the acquisition of marital property and to the husband’s professional career. The husband obtained a medical license during the marriage, which significantly enhanced his earning capacity. Although the license itself is not marital property subject to distribution, the court must consider the wife’s contributions to the development of that enhanced earning capacity when determining an equitable distribution of marital assets. The duration of the marriage and the parties’ respective financial circumstances further support an unequal distribution in favor of the wife.
